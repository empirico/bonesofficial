After two years from their first self-produced EP, spent sweating on stage and stressing valves and skins to their extreme, BONES & COMFORT are proud to announce the release of their first album, MOTHERSHEEP.

Recorded and mastered by Gigione Galmozzi at his Morbid Sound Studio, MOTHERSHEEP contains 10 riff-filled tracks and a fusion of southern rock and stoner.
A forty-six minutes relentless ride, driven by intricate riffs and seventies grooves: MOTHERSHEEP has been released on 20th of March by GO DOWN RECORDS.
